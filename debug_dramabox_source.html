Between Lies and Love(DUBBED) Episode 79. Determined to tear apart Emma Boyer's marriage, Penny Chandler orchestrates a meeting between Emma and a stranger, hoping to drive the couple to divorce. However, Emma is unexpectedly rescued by Steve Chandler, who becomes her savior. As their bond deepens, Emma discovers she is pregnant with Steve’s only child.
